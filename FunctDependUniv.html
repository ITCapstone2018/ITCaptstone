Identifying Functional Dependencies : Parking on University Campus

A university is designing a system to track parking for faculty, staff, and students. Everyone at the university pays for the right to park and is then assigned a zone in which he or she may park. These zones have unique pricing - zones with parking spaces closer to campus buildings cost more than zones further away. Additionally, zoning restrictions are in place based on one's role at the university. Zone A is reserved for the University President and College Deans. Faculty can park in any zone except B or C. Staff can park in only Zones D or E, and students can park in Zones F-H.

Given the following attributes identify the most likely functional dependencies:

University_ID, University_position, Parking_zone, Parking_space, Parking_price

A

University_positions determines University_ID, Parking-zone

Parking_zone determines Parking_space, Parking_price

B

University_ID determines Parking_space

Parking_space determines Parking_zone

Parking_zone determines Parking_price

C

University_ID determines University_position, Parking_zone

Parking_space determines Parking_zone, Parking_price
